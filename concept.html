All of the Concept Art I've Made
What is Showcased? Description
Just General Art for the Game This piece of concept art depicts just a general selection of characters. I sort of wanted to just try and make art for the game in general so that I could visualize some sprites out onto paper.
King Paul's Castle The actual castle would have two cannons on either side. The concept art just sort of looks like a bong. Aside from that the idea i had it just sort of a tower with a blob of slime at the top and two cannons on either side to shoot slime across the area in an attemptto claim it.
King Paul Himself This art was made by my friend Joel, this was the original concept for King Paul that I used as reference for the actual sprite. He was telling me I should make some sort of big Glorp character to fight and I told him I actually wanted to do that. The only issue is it just wouldn't look right. He then sent me this picture to put what he was thinking out onto paper and the design stuck ever since.
Paul Rematch There is a lot of concept art related to King Paul but this is basically the concept art that I made to depict the planned rematch against him. In this rematch he was kicked out of the castle and forced to live in the garbage dump with his only followers left: the Red Glorps.
Enemies I don't really know what more to say about this. This is just concept art for enemies. Not all of them have been made in sprite form yet but I've made quite a few.
Rap Battle So basically in this I am having a rap battle with evil Kervin and Ghoulo as a DJ in the background. This is planned to be a reference to the game FNF as just a really complicated one-off joke.
The Nucleator This was my original concept for the final boss of the game. I still made this into a sprite but when I showed it to the now recurring person Joel, he just said that it didn't work because the character didn't feel relevent to the rest of the game and that there should be a sort of twist final boss afterwards.
Mutant Larry This final piece of concept art is what I actually made after Joel's critisism. Larry is a character on your team for the whole game, but when he falls into a barrel of nuclear waste he transforms into this monstrosity. In this concept art I also added the character CoolGuy11 at the bottom as he was a character whow anted to join the party for the entire game. Once Larry's gone he has full reign to take his spot in battle.
