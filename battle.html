The Battle System

Battles in this game are planned to be sort of a mix between games like Deltarune and the Mario and Luigi Series. The idea is: you select three of eight characters to be in your party. All of these characters have varying stats. I also wanna add a secret unlockable character.

The Steps of a Battle

  1. When a battle starts, your three of eight chosen characters will take turns in the order that you set them.
  2. Once you've chosen what all three characters will do they will attack and then each enemy turn one character will go out to dodge enemy attacks in chronological order.
  3. While dodging, some character will have abilities unique to them like how some party members may be able to dodge, block, or dash.
  4. When all enemies are defeated the three chosen characters will gain the appropriate amount of exp based on what enemies were fought.
  5. On rare occasions, bossfight may work differently, I haven't fully planned things out but I'd like one fight to have a 3D moving background at a low pixelated resolution where you walk around a volcano dodging attacks. There's also another fight that would have the players facing up at the boss looming over them as opposed to a more side-based view.
